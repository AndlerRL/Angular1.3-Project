Lonely Road Fair 2017

Official Anual Motocicle Fair of the Region.
Welcome to our fair website! As our first year of our bikes presentation, we shall have available the best motocicles with some of our providers. As a first year, we shall have 3 standard bikes types and a show up of them. Do not leave this out!








  • The Lonely Road it has been a proyect thought for our bikes fans; Show ups, activities, food are some aspects that we shall have at our visitor's disposition! As tradition that we desire to build among this fair, we will be available in this next month of January, opening the year with the world's most refreshing bikes, remembering you the passion of them every year.

  • In just 3 days you will meet the best that we can offer and within this year, we bring you a lot of fun:

    On the 1st day, you'll see the entrance of our bikers, making the aperture of the fair with fascination races, show up and even the circle of death! And also opening a path to the bikes that we will present you for the rest of the fair days.

    On the 2nd day, it will arrive our collectionist, better yet, the crazy bikers, whom bring presentation of exclusive bikes designs, also, for the collectionist, some authenticies that you ever will find in another place.

    On the last 3rd day, there will be available higher proffessional league bike runners bringing authographs and cheering up our fans. With them, you can join them in a big buffet brought not only by us, also for one of our collaborators in thin fair. C'on, don't losse this chance and visit us!


  • Nevertheless, the Lonely Road Fair is commemored in memory of Peters Fellstrong, who has been a great example for this big collabolators and founder of this magnificent event, witch has been develop thanks to him.


  • Peters Fellstrong was a bike runner, daredevil and bike fanatic of the 70's, that brough us too much joy on his shows, acrobacies that let the public with pale skin and adrenaline on every event that he did. In 1978 had his Platinium Hipper Adrenaline Medal for doing one of the most deadly acrobacies of the world, the 'Death jump with chains and headed strong-box with a tank full of electric-ninja-sharks', that for our generation sound creazy, but terrifying to see. Fellstrong died on the act, but before it, he left his legacy in our hearts for decades and inspiration that still beating hard.
